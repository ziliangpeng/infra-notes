### Sweet-Spot Search: Job 201934 (gcp5-h100-0-3)

Binary search to find model size where none-AC max_bs = 1~2.
Architecture: d_model=3072, n_heads=24, n_kv_heads=8, d_ff=8192 (same family as 3B, more layers).

| 34       | 3.52B   | bs=1/2/3 OK -> too easy |

### 3.62B Custom Model AC Benchmark: Job 201953 (gcp5-h100-0-3)

Architecture: d_model=3072, n_layers=35, n_heads=24, n_kv_heads=8, d_ff=8192, ~3.62B
